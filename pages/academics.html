Schools & Centres

Our Faculties serve as pillars of academic excellence, overseeing a diverse array of Schools and Centres. Each Faculty is a hub of innovation, offering programs that blend rigorous academics with practical, real-world applications.

Engineering, Applied Sciences and Technology

The Faculty of Engineering, Applied Sciences, and Technology (FEAST) at Shobhit University incorporates a comprehensive and multidisciplinary structure, encompassing a wide range of fields within its Schools and Centres. Each School and Centre under FEAST has a specific focus, reflecting the diverse academic and research pursuits within the fields of engineering, applied sciences, and technology. Here's an outline of each School and Centre, along with their potential mandates:


School of Computational Sciences and Engineering

Focus: Specializes in computer science, software engineering, data analytics, and related fields.

Courses Offered: B.Tech (CSE), M.Tech (CSE), BCA, MCA, B.Sc (CS), M.Sc (CS), Ph.D.

Business & Entrepreneurship

The Faculty of Business & Entrepreneurship at SU encapsulates a dynamic range of disciplines focusing on business education, psychological aspects of business, and the integration of technology in commerce and economics. This Faculty is structured to provide both broad and specialized knowledge in business and its interdisciplinary connections, through its school and centres:


School of Business Studies

Focus: Provides business education with specializations in finance, marketing, human resource management, and entrepreneurship.

Courses Offered: MBA, BBA, Ph.D.

Legal Studies (FLS)

The Faculty of Legal Studies (FLS) at SU is dedicated to the comprehensive study and practice of law, focusing on both the theoretical foundations of legal systems and the practical applications of law in governance, policy, and society. Here's a breakdown of the School and Centre within FLS, highlighting their potential focuses and mandates:


School of Law

Focus: This School specializes in the study of law with a particular emphasis on constitutional law, exploring the principles and functioning of constitutional systems, civil rights, and the role of law in structuring society and government.

Courses Offered: LLB, LLM, Ph.D.

Teaching & Learning Sciences (FTLS)

The Faculty of Teaching & Learning Sciences (FTLS) at SU is focused on advancing the fields of education and learning, from the foundational theories and practices of teaching to ensuring equitable access to education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities. Here's an overview of the School and Centre within the FTLS and their potential objectives:


School of Education (SEdu)

Focus: The School of Education is primarily concerned with the study and practice of education, encompassing a wide range of topics from teaching methodologies, curriculum development, educational psychology, to the administration and policy aspects of education systems.

Courses Offered: BA, MA, Ph.D.
